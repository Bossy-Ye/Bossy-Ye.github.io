Academic Homepage

Boshuai Ye

PhD Researcher in Quantum Software Engineering
University of Oulu

I study trustworthy quantum software and rigorous evidence for quantum claims, with a focus on reproducibility science, empirical software engineering methods, and practical reliability in quantum systems. I also contribute to C2|Q> through parser-focused tooling and workflow design for traceable classical-to-quantum translation.

Research Themes

Production-Grade Quantum Software

Building testable, maintainable, and versioned quantum code with strict engineering practices for real-world research workflows.

Classical-to-Quantum Pipeline Engineering

Implementing end-to-end toolchains that parse classical problem specifications and generate executable quantum circuits.

Executable Reproducibility

Turning papers into runnable artifacts with pinned dependencies, clear pipelines, and transparent evaluation protocols.

Verification of Quantum Claims

Stress-testing reported quantum results with replication scripts, validation checks, and cross-environment execution before claims are accepted.
