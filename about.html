About

I am a PhD Researcher at the University of Oulu. My research focuses on trustworthy quantum software workflows across the classical-to-quantum bridge and heterogeneous computing infrastructures. I study how compilation and mapping effects, implementation variability, execution uncertainty, and resource constraints shape workflow behavior, empirical reliability, and the validity of software-level interpretations. By combining empirical software engineering, validation-centric design, and systems-level analysis, I aim to make quantum software more auditable, reproducible, and technically robust under realistic execution conditions.

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy quantum software workflows across the classical-to-quantum bridge
  • Compilation, mapping, and execution effects in heterogeneous infrastructures
  • Empirical reliability and validity of software-level interpretations
  • Reproducible and auditable validation for quantum software studies
