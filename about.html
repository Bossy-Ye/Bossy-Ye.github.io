About

I am a PhD Researcher in Quantum Software Engineering at the University of Oulu. My work focuses on how to make quantum software and quantum computing claims more trustworthy through empirical methods, reproducibility-oriented evaluation, and engineering discipline.

Research Interests

  • Trustworthy quantum software development and validation
  • Stability and credibility assessment of quantum claims
  • Reproducibility science for quantum computing studies
  • Empirical software engineering methods for quantum systems
